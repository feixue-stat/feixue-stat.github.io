About Me


I'm an Assistant Professor of Statistics at Purdue University. Prior to joining purdue, I was a Postdoc Researcher under the supervision of Professor Hongzhe Li in Department of Biostatistics, Epidemiology and Informatics at University of Pennsylvania. I received my PhD degree in Department of Statistics at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2019, advised by Professor Annie Qu. Prior to UIUC, I obtained my bachelor’s degree from School of Mathematical Sciences at Fudan University in 2014.


I am interested in data integration, mobile health, missing data, high dimensional inference, and statistical genetics. Here is my CV.


Our group has recently become interested in AI/LLM for Health. If you are interested in collaborating with us, please feel free to contact me.


My work is partially supported by National Science Foundation (NSF) grant DMS 2210860 (sole PI).


I am recruiting PhD students in statistics. Please email me if you are highly motivated and passionate about development of novel data-driven statistical methods or applications.
